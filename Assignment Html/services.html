Library Services

The Campus Library offers a variety of services to support students and staff in their academic activities.

The Campus Library is committed to providing high-quality services that support teaching, learning, and research ,
within the campus community. Our services are designed to meet the academic needs of students from different disciplines.
In addition to physical resources, the library offers access to online materials that can be used both on campus and remotely.
These services ensure that students have continuous access to learning resources.

  • Book lending and returns
  • Access to academic journals
  • Digital and online resources
  • Quiet study areas
  • Research assistance
  • Internet and computer access
